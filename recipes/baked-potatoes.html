Perfect Baked Potatoes

Image attribution: “Baked Potato” by Pixabay, CC0 1.0 Universal

Description

Serves 2-4

Get perfect baked potatoes with a crispy skin and a fluffy interior every time!

This also works well in a toaster oven or air fryer!

Ingredients

  • 2 russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil (use one suitable for high heat, like grapeseed, canola, or avocado)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Optional toppings: butter, sour cream, chives, smoked salmon, bacon, cheese, broccoli, chili.

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 450° (about 232° C) or your air fryer to 400° F (about 205° C)).
  2. Meanwhile, rub each potato with oil and sprinkle evenly with salt.
  3. Place the potatoes on a wire rack over a baking sheet, or directly onto your toaster oven rack or air fryer basket.
  4. Bake until the skin is crispy and the interior is soft, 45-60 minutes in an oven or toaster oven or 35-45 minutes in an air fryer. When the potato is done, it will "give" when squeezed gently, or read 205-210° F (about 96-99° C) on an instant-read thermometer.
  5. Remove from oven and cut open immediately to allow steam to escape.
  6. Serve with your favorite toppings.
