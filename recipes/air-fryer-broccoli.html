Air Fryer Roasted Broccoli

Description

Serves 2-4

Roasting broccoli gives it a sweet and complex flavor, and this three-ingredient recipe comes together in minutes.

For more punch, sprinkle generiously with spices before roasting. My favorites are garlic powder, red pepper flakes, and cajun seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 1 large broccoli crown, chopped into equally-sized florets
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Pre-heat air fryer to 400° F (about 205° C).
  2. Meanwhile, drizzle the broccoli with olive oil, sprinkle with salt, and toss to coat evenly.
  3. Once the air fryer has pre-heated, add the broccoli, and set timer for 6 minutes.
  4. Cook, tossing the basket or turning the pieces once or twice. If some pieces are getting dark, move them towards the center of the basket or remove them early.
  5. Remove from heat, and add the stuffing mix. Stir to combine. If it still feels dry, gradually add extra stock or water.
  6. Add additional salt to taste, if desired.
